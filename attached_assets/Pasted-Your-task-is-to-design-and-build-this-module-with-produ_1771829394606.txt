• Fetch ads performance data
• Store it in database
• Calculate profitability
• Show reports dashboard
• Filter by date ranges
• Track active campaigns live

--------------------------------------------------

Integrate Meta Marketing API using:

1) Ad Accounts
2) Campaigns
3) Ad Sets
4) Ads
5) Insights Metrics
